Who are we & What we do?


  • We at Actiwate, a two year old organisation headquartered in Delhi are trying to make organizing events hassle free. The journey started with Amanjot Malhotra realizing that a simple tech solution can make life quite easy for the users and event organizers out there. We although bootstrapped, are kept afloat for the past two years because of our will.

  • Today, we have built a robust system which is competitive and at the same time very easy to use. Moreover we have a very strong understanding of the market and the consumer requirements, which we will continue to fulfill as we grow.

  • At this point we have strong technical resources in addition to an efficient business team, which is helping us grow our market share rapidly.
